Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

Iconic Luxury Resort with Stunning Skyline Views

About the Attraction

Marina Bay Sands is Singapore's most recognizable landmark, featuring three 55-story towers topped by a spectacular sky park. This integrated resort offers a luxury hotel, high-end shopping mall, world-class casino, museum, theaters, and the famous infinity pool with breathtaking views of the city skyline.

Key Features

  • Infinity pool (for hotel guests only)
  • Sands SkyPark Observation Deck
  • The Shoppes at Marina Bay Sands (luxury mall)
  • ArtScience Museum
  • Spectra light and water show

Visitor Information

  • Opening hours: 24/7 (various facilities have different hours)
  • SkyPark Observation Deck ticket: ~SGD 23
  • Dress code: Smart casual for casino and some restaurants
  • Best time to visit: Evening for city lights
  • Photography allowed except in casino

Getting There

  • MRT: Bayfront Station (CE1/DT16)
  • Bus services: 97, 106, 133, 502
  • 10-minute walk from Raffles Place MRT

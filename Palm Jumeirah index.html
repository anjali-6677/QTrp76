Palm Jumeirah Experience

World's Largest Man-Made Island

About the Island

Palm Jumeirah is an artificial archipelago shaped like a palm tree, extending into the Persian Gulf. This engineering marvel features luxury hotels including Atlantis The Palm, private beaches, upscale residences, and world-class dining. The island's crescent acts as a breakwater and is home to some of Dubai's most exclusive beach clubs and resorts.

Experience Highlights

  • Atlantis The Palm resort and Aquaventure Waterpark
  • Private beach clubs and luxury resorts
  • The Pointe dining and entertainment district
  • Palm West Beach public beach area
  • Monorail with views of the palm

Know before you go

  • Accessible by car, monorail, or water taxi
  • Beach clubs require advance reservations
  • Public beaches available with limited facilities
  • Best views from helicopter tours or Skydive Dubai
  • Parking available but limited in peak seasons

Not included

  • Resort day passes or hotel stays
  • Waterpark tickets
  • Dining expenses
  • Transportation to the island

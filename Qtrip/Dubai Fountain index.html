Dubai Fountain Experience

World's Largest Choreographed Fountain System

About the Fountain

The Dubai Fountain is set on the 30-acre Burj Khalifa Lake, performing daily shows synchronized to classical, Arabic, and world music. Designed by the creators of the Bellagio Fountains in Las Vegas, it shoots water up to 500 feet in the air (equivalent to a 50-story building) illuminated by 6,600 lights and 25 colored projectors.

Show Highlights

  • Daily performances every 30 minutes from 6PM to 11PM
  • Afternoon shows at 1PM and 1:30PM (1:30PM and 2PM on Fridays)
  • Boardwalk experience bringing you just 9m from fountains
  • Traditional Abra boat rides during shows
  • Views from multiple vantage points around the lake

Know before you go

  • Free to watch from public areas
  • Boardwalk tickets available for closer experience
  • Best views from Dubai Mall terrace or Souk Al Bahar
  • Evening shows most popular - arrive early
  • Combined with Burj Khalifa visit for full experience

Not included

  • Abra boat rides (additional charge)
  • Boardwalk access tickets
  • Dining at surrounding restaurants
  • Professional photography

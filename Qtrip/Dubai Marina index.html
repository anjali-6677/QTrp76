Dubai Marina Experience

The Manhattan of the Middle East

About the Marina

Dubai Marina is an artificial canal city built along a 3-kilometer stretch of Persian Gulf shoreline. This luxurious district features the world's largest man-made marina, surrounded by towering skyscrapers, upscale hotels, and the Marina Walk promenade lined with cafes and restaurants. The area comes alive at night with its illuminated skyscrapers reflecting on the marina waters.

Experience Highlights

  • Marina Walk with waterfront dining options
  • Dhow cruise and yacht rental experiences
  • Skydive Dubai for breathtaking views
  • Marina Mall for luxury shopping
  • Pier 7 with seven floors of restaurants

Know before you go

  • Best explored in the evening when temperatures cool
  • Marina Walk stretches 7km - wear comfortable shoes
  • Water taxi available for marina transport
  • Parking available but limited in peak times
  • Nearby metro station: Dubai Marina (Red Line)

Not included

  • Dhow cruise or yacht rental fees
  • Dining expenses at waterfront restaurants
  • Skydiving or other adventure activities
  • Personal shopping expenses

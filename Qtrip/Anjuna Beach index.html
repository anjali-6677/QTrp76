Anjuna Beach

Goa's Famous Hippie Beach

About the Beach

Anjuna Beach is one of Goa's most famous beaches, known for its white sandy shores, rocky outcrops, and vibrant flea market. Once the epicenter of hippie culture in the 1960s and 70s, it still retains its bohemian charm with beach shacks, trance parties, and a relaxed atmosphere.

Experience Highlights

  • Weekly Wednesday Flea Market with handicrafts and souvenirs
  • Beautiful rocky cliffs and white sand beaches
  • Famous beach shacks serving fresh seafood
  • Full moon parties and electronic music events
  • Water sports like parasailing and jet skiing

Know before you go

  • Best time to visit: November to March
  • Market day: Every Wednesday (8AM to 6PM)
  • Beach shacks open from 9AM to midnight
  • Limited parking available near the beach
  • Watch out for strong currents during monsoon

Not included

  • Water sports activities
  • Food and drinks at shacks
  • Market purchases

Divine Serenity Temple

Find Peace and Spiritual Awakening

About the Experience

Divine Serenity Temple is a place of peace and devotion, set in a tranquil environment that soothes the soul. The temple complex spans over several acres, offering a serene retreat for visitors. Participate in daily prayers, meditate in the quiet sanctuaries, or explore the intricate carvings and sacred texts. Engage in devotional music sessions and witness mesmerizing evening aartis that uplift the spirit.

Inclusions

  • Entry to the temple premises
  • Participation in daily aarti and prayer ceremonies
  • Access to meditation halls and spiritual discourses
  • Free guided temple tour
  • Parking

Know before you go

  • Proper traditional attire is required for entry
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas
  • Footwear must be removed before entering the temple
  • Children must be accompanied by parents or guardians at all times
  • Entry is free for children below the height of three feet
  • A valid photo ID is mandatory to enter the temple

Not included

  • Special puja offerings
  • Pickup and drop
  • Personal prayer items

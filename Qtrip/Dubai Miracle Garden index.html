Miracle Garden Experience

World's Largest Natural Flower Garden

About the Garden

Dubai Miracle Garden spans over 72,000 square meters featuring more than 150 million flowers arranged in stunning displays and structures. Open seasonally from November to April, this floral wonderland includes life-sized houses, heart-shaped arches, and even an Emirates A380 airplane covered entirely in flowers. The garden recycles wastewater through a drip irrigation system to maintain its blooms in Dubai's desert climate.

Garden Highlights

  • Floral castles and Disney Avenue
  • Butterfly Garden (world's largest indoor butterfly garden)
  • Emirates A380 floral installation
  • Umbrella tunnel and heart-shaped pathways
  • Seasonal themed displays

Know before you go

  • Open seasonally (November to April)
  • Operating hours: 9AM to 9PM weekdays, 9AM to 11PM weekends
  • Best time to visit: Early morning or late afternoon
  • Photography allowed throughout
  • Combined tickets available with Butterfly Garden

Not included

  • Butterfly Garden entry (separate ticket)
  • Food and beverages
  • Transportation to the garden
  • Guided tours (available for additional fee)

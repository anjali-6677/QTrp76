Klang Valley

Greater Kuala Lumpur's Urban and Cultural Hub

About the Region

Klang Valley encompasses Kuala Lumpur and its sprawling suburbs, offering diverse experiences from modern megamalls to historic royal towns. This economic heartland of Malaysia combines urban attractions with pockets of nature, cultural landmarks, and some of the country's best food.

Key Attractions

  • Putrajaya (modern administrative capital)
  • Sunway Lagoon theme park
  • Royal town of Klang (famous for bak kut teh)
  • Batu Caves (outside KL proper)
  • FRIM Forest Research Institute

Travel Tips

  • Traffic can be heavy - plan travel times
  • Weekends see locals flocking to malls
  • Try different regional food specialties
  • Use metro system to avoid traffic
  • Many attractions are kid-friendly

Getting There

  • Same access as Kuala Lumpur (KLIA airport)
  • Extensive highway network
  • KTM commuter trains connect suburbs

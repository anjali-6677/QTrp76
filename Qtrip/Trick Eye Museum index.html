Trick Eye Museum, Singapore

Interactive 3D Art Experience

About the Attraction

The Trick Eye Museum at Sentosa is an interactive 3D art museum where visitors become part of optical illusion artworks. Using augmented reality technology and clever perspective tricks, it creates fun photo opportunities where you can appear to be riding a dragon, floating in space, or escaping quicksand.

Key Features

  • Six themed zones with 80+ installations
  • AR technology enhances the experience
  • Interactive props provided
  • Special lighting effects
  • Free digital photos with admission

Visitor Information

  • Opening hours: 10am-9pm daily
  • Ticket prices: SGD 25 (adults), SGD 20 (children)
  • Last admission at 8pm
  • Allow 1-2 hours for visit
  • Wear bright colored clothes for best photos

Getting There

  • Location: Resorts World Sentosa
  • Sentosa Express to Waterfront Station
  • Bus services: RWS8, 123, 188R
  • 10-minute walk from Beach Station

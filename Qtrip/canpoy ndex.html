Rainforest Canopy Adventure

Soar And Explore The Treetops

About the Experience

Experience the thrill of soaring through the rainforest canopy on our award-winning adventure course. This eco-adventure features a series of zip lines, suspension bridges, and aerial walkways that take you high above the forest floor. Our certified guides will ensure your safety as you navigate through the treetops, offering insights about the unique ecosystem and wildlife that call the canopy home. Suitable for beginners and adrenaline seekers alike!

Inclusions

  • Full safety equipment (harness, helmet, gloves)
  • Certified canopy tour guides
  • Access to 12 zip lines and 8 canopy bridges
  • Safety orientation and training
  • Complimentary locker for personal items

Know before you go

  • Minimum age: 10 years
  • Maximum weight limit: 250 lbs (113 kg)
  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • Not recommended for those with fear of heights
  • Activity lasts approximately 3 hours

Not included

  • Transportation to the canopy site
  • Meals and beverages
  • Photos and videos (available for purchase)
  • Gratuities for guides

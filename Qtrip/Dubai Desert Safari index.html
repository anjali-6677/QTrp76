Desert Safari Experience

Authentic Arabian Adventure

About the Safari

The Dubai desert safari offers an exhilarating escape into the golden sands of the Arabian desert. Experience dune bashing in 4x4 vehicles, camel rides, sandboarding, and a traditional Bedouin-style camp with live entertainment. As the sun sets over the dunes, enjoy a barbecue dinner under the stars with belly dancing and Tanoura performances.

Safari Highlights

  • Dune bashing in air-conditioned 4x4 vehicles
  • Camel riding and sandboarding
  • Traditional Arabic welcome at desert camp
  • Live entertainment including belly dancing
  • Unlimited refreshments and BBQ dinner

Know before you go

  • Morning and evening safaris available
  • Pickup/drop-off included from Dubai hotels
  • Wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes
  • Not recommended for pregnant women or back problems
  • Evening safaris include sunset photography stop

Not included

  • Quad biking (available for additional fee)
  • Alcoholic beverages (available for purchase)
  • Henna painting and souvenir photos
  • Private VIP experiences

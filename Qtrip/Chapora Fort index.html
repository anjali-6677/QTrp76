Chapora Fort

The Dil Chahta Hai Fort

About the Fort

Chapora Fort, made famous by the Bollywood movie 'Dil Chahta Hai', is a 17th-century Portuguese fort located atop a hill overlooking the Chapora River. Though mostly in ruins now, the fort offers spectacular panoramic views of Vagator Beach, Anjuna Beach, and the Arabian Sea. The red laterite stone walls and strategic location make it a popular sunset spot.

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple

Seek Blessings Of Lord Ganesha

About the Temple

The Shree Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple in Mumbai is one of India's most revered temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha. Built in 1801, this temple attracts millions of devotees each year who come to seek the blessings of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. The temple's magnificent architecture, spiritual atmosphere, and the powerful idol of Lord Ganesha carved from a single black stone make it a must-visit spiritual destination.

Darshan Inclusions

  • General darshan (when available)
  • Special darshan for senior citizens and differently-abled
  • Online booking facility for specific time slots
  • Prasadam distribution after darshan
  • Access to temple museum and information center

Know before you go

  • Temple timings: 5:30 AM to 9:30 PM
  • Dress modestly (shoulders and knees covered)
  • Mobile phones and cameras not allowed inside sanctum
  • Expect long queues during festivals and weekends
  • Security check mandatory for all visitors

Not allowed

  • Leather items (belts, wallets, etc.)
  • Outside food and drinks
  • Large bags and backpacks
  • Smoking and alcohol strictly prohibited

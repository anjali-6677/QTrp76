Grand Bazaar Adventure

Discover And Shop Local Treasures

About the Experience

Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of the Grand Bazaar, one of the world's oldest and largest covered markets. With over 4,000 shops spread across 61 streets, this historic marketplace offers an unforgettable shopping experience. Our guided tour takes you through the maze of colorful stalls selling everything from handcrafted jewelry and textiles to spices and antiques. Experience the sights, sounds, and aromas of this bustling marketplace while learning about its 550-year history.

Inclusions

  • Expert local guide fluent in multiple languages
  • Historical introduction to the bazaar's architecture
  • Visits to authentic artisan workshops
  • Tasting of local delicacies
  • Bargaining tips and shopping advice

Know before you go

  • Tours last approximately 3 hours
  • Comfortable walking shoes essential
  • Market is closed on Sundays
  • Cash is preferred by most vendors
  • Bargaining is expected and encouraged

Not included

  • Personal shopping expenses
  • Transportation to/from the bazaar
  • Additional food and drinks beyond tasting samples
  • Gratuities for your guide (optional)

Calangute Beach

The Queen of Beaches in Goa

About the Beach

Calangute Beach is the largest beach in North Goa, stretching from Candolim to Baga. Known as the "Queen of Beaches," it offers golden sands, exciting water sports, and a lively atmosphere with numerous shacks, shops, and entertainment options along the shore.

Experience Highlights

  • Wide range of water sports including jet skiing and parasailing
  • Numerous beach shacks serving Goan cuisine
  • Vibrant nightlife with beach parties
  • Shopping for souvenirs and beachwear
  • Ayurvedic massages available on the beach

Know before you go

  • Most crowded between December and January
  • Water sports available from 9AM to sunset
  • Best time for photography: Early morning
  • Bargain for prices on water sports and souvenirs
  • Watch your belongings in crowded areas

Not included

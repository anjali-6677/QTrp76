Seine River Cruise

Paris from the Water

About the Cruise

See Paris's iconic landmarks from a unique perspective on a scenic boat tour along the Seine River. These 1-hour cruises pass under historic bridges and offer unparalleled views of Notre Dame, the Eiffel Tower, Louvre, and more, with live or recorded commentary.

Cruise Highlights

  • Views of 20+ Paris landmarks
  • Pass under 15 historic bridges
  • Daytime and evening cruise options
  • Dinner cruise options available
  • Open-air or glass-enclosed boats

Know before you go

  • Departures every 30-60 minutes from multiple docks
  • Evening cruises offer illuminated city views
  • Audio guides available in multiple languages
  • Best photo spots on upper deck
  • Combination tickets with other attractions available

Not included

  • Meals (unless dinner cruise option selected)
  • Hotel pickup/drop-off
  • Premium seating upgrades
  • Private boat charters

Notre Dame Cathedral

Masterpiece of French Gothic Architecture

About the Cathedral

Construction began in 1163 and took nearly 200 years to complete. This medieval Catholic cathedral is renowned for its size, antiquity, and architectural interest. Though damaged by fire in 2019, restoration efforts are underway to preserve this iconic landmark.

Architectural Highlights

  • Spectacular rose windows (13th century)
  • Gothic flying buttresses
  • Historic organ with 8,000 pipes
  • The Gallery of Kings (28 statues)
  • Gargoyles and chimera decorations

Know before you go

  • Currently under restoration (exterior viewing only)
  • Located on Île de la Cité in the Seine River
  • Nearby attractions: Sainte-Chapelle, Shakespeare & Company
  • Best viewed from Square Jean XXIII park
  • Evening illumination shows the cathedral's details

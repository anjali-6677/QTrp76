Sky Tower Adventure

Reach new heights and see the world from above

About the Experience

Experience a breathtaking view of the city from the top of the Sky Tower. Walk on the glass-floored observation deck, feel the thrill of open-air viewing, and immerse yourself in a VR city tour to explore the past and future of the skyline.

Inclusions

  • Access to the 360-degree observation deck
  • Glass-floor skywalk experience
  • VR city tour
  • Guided history tour

Know before you go

  • Entry tickets must be purchased in advance
  • Photography is allowed, but tripods are prohibited
  • Children must be accompanied by adults
  • Security screening is mandatory before entry
  • No outside food and beverages are allowed

Not included

  • Private guide
  • Food and beverages
  • Transportation

Gardens by the Bay, Singapore

Futuristic Nature Park with Spectacular Super Trees

About the Attraction

Gardens by the Bay is a 101-hectare nature park featuring futuristic Supertree structures, massive climate-controlled conservatories, and beautifully landscaped gardens. This award-winning horticultural attraction showcases sustainable practices while offering stunning visual experiences.

Key Features

  • Supertree Grove (12 iconic vertical gardens)
  • Flower Dome (world's largest glass greenhouse)
  • Cloud Forest (35m tall mountain with waterfall)
  • OCBC Skyway (aerial walkway between Supertrees)
  • Garden Rhapsody light and sound show

Visitor Information

  • Opening hours: 5am-2am (outdoor gardens), 9am-9pm (conservatories)
  • Ticket prices: SGD 28 for conservatories
  • Free admission to outdoor gardens
  • Best time to visit: Early morning or evening
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes

Getting There

  • MRT: Bayfront Station (CE1/DT16)
  • Free shuttle from Marina Bay Sands
  • Bus services: 400, 402, 518

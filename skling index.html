Snowpeak Skiing

Thrill And Adventure On The Slopes

About the Experience

Snowpeak Skiing Resort offers an exhilarating winter sports experience with slopes suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced skiers. Our resort features over 50 kilometers of well-groomed ski runs, state-of-the-art ski lifts, and breathtaking mountain views. Whether you're looking to learn the basics or challenge yourself on black diamond runs, our professional instructors and top-notch facilities will ensure an unforgettable skiing adventure.

Inclusions

  • Ski lift access for all runs
  • Professional ski equipment (skis, poles, boots)
  • Safety gear (helmet, goggles)
  • Basic skiing lessons for beginners
  • Access to warming huts and lodges

Know before you go

  • All participants must sign a waiver before skiing
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Proper winter clothing is required (not provided)
  • Ski runs are subject to weather conditions
  • Advanced booking recommended for ski lessons

Not included

  • Ski clothing (jackets, pants, gloves)
  • Private lessons
  • Food and beverages
  • Accommodation

Prestige University Tour

Explore And Expand Your Knowledge

About the Experience

Prestige University offers an immersive campus tour experience that showcases our world-class facilities, historic architecture, and vibrant student life. Founded in 1890, our institution combines tradition with innovation, offering visitors a glimpse into academic excellence. The tour includes visits to our renowned library, state-of-the-art laboratories, student centers, and athletic facilities, giving prospective students and visitors a comprehensive view of university life.

Inclusions

  • Guided campus tour with current students
  • Access to academic buildings and facilities
  • Information session with admissions staff
  • Student life presentation
  • Complimentary campus map and information packet

Know before you go

  • Tours last approximately 2 hours
  • Comfortable walking shoes recommended
  • Photography is permitted in most areas
  • Visitors must check in at the admissions office
  • Group tours require advance registration

Not included

  • Meals or refreshments
  • Parking fees (discounts available for visitors)
  • Overnight accommodations
  • Classroom visits (available by special request)

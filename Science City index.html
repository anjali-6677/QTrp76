Science City Kolkata

Explore and Experience the Wonders of Science

About the Experience

Science City Kolkata is one of the largest and most dynamic science centers in India, offering an engaging experience for visitors of all ages. The facility spans a vast area and features interactive exhibits, digital planetarium shows, and thrilling science-based activities. Visitors can explore the Earth Exploration Hall, ride the Ropeway, or witness the wonders of space in the Space Theatre. For those keen on robotics and virtual reality, there are plenty of hands-on exhibits to enjoy.

Inclusions

  • Entry to all major science exhibits
  • Access to the Digital Planetarium
  • Ride on the Ropeway for a scenic view
  • Virtual reality and robotics experiences
  • Parking

Know before you go

  • Some exhibits and shows may require an additional ticket
  • All exhibits and rides are subject to availability
  • Proper attire must be worn for specific rides
  • Children must be accompanied by parents or guardians at all times
  • Complimentary entry for children below the height of three feet
  • A valid photo ID is mandatory to enter Science City

Not included

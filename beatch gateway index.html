About the Experience

Escape to our award-winning beach resort, where golden sands meet crystal-clear waters. This tropical paradise offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Spend your days lounging under palm trees, swimming in the warm ocean, or trying exciting water sports. Our beachfront location features luxurious amenities, delicious dining options, and breathtaking sunsets that will create memories to last a lifetime.

Inclusions

  • Beachfront accommodation with ocean views
  • Daily breakfast buffet
  • Access to swimming pools and private beach area
  • Complimentary non-motorized water sports (snorkeling, kayaking)
  • Beach chairs, umbrellas, and towels

Know before you go

  • Check-in: 3 PM | Check-out: 11 AM
  • Beach attire appropriate for all public areas
  • Reef-safe sunscreen recommended
  • Reservations required for spa services and specialty dining
  • Children's programs available seasonally

Not included

  • Airfare and airport transfers
  • Spa treatments and premium activities
  • Premium alcoholic beverages
  • Motorized water sports (available for additional fee)

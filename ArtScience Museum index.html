ArtScience Museum, Singapore

Where Art, Science, Culture and Technology Collide

About the Attraction

The lotus-inspired ArtScience Museum at Marina Bay Sands is Singapore's premier cultural institution exploring the creative processes at the heart of art, science, technology and culture. Its interactive exhibitions combine cutting-edge technology with artistic expression in a stunning architectural masterpiece.

Key Features

  • Permanent Future World exhibition (digital art)
  • Rotating international exhibitions
  • Innovative interactive installations
  • Striking lotus-inspired architecture
  • VR and AR experiences

Visitor Information

  • Opening hours: 10am-7pm daily
  • Ticket prices: SGD 19-38 depending on exhibitions
  • Free admission for children under 2
  • Photography allowed (no flash)
  • Last admission at 6pm

Getting There

  • MRT: Bayfront Station (CE1/DT16)
  • Connected to Marina Bay Sands
  • Bus services: 97, 106, 133, 502
  • 10-minute walk from Raffles Place MRT

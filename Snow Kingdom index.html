Snow Kingdom

Mumbai's Only Snow Park Experience

About the Experience

Snow Kingdom is India's largest snow park spread across 13,000 sq.ft. of real snow at a constant temperature of -10Â°C. Located in Powai, this winter wonderland offers a magical escape from Mumbai's tropical climate with exciting snow activities, thrilling slides, and picturesque snow landscapes. Perfect for families and friends looking for a unique winter experience without leaving the city.

Attractions Included

  • Multiple snow slides (Tube Slide, Snow Bob Slide)
  • Snow play zone with snowball fights and igloos
  • Ice skating rink (seasonal)
  • Snowfall experience every 30 minutes
  • Photo opportunities with snow sculptures

Know before you go

  • Operating hours: 10:30 AM to 9:30 PM (last entry at 8:30 PM)
  • Winter jackets and boots provided (included in ticket)
  • Recommended to wear full-length pants and socks
  • Session duration: 1 hour (includes changing time)
  • Children below 3 years enter free

Not included

  • Gloves (available for purchase)
  • Food and beverages
  • Locker rentals (additional charge)
  • Professional photographs (available for purchase)

Eiffel Tower

Iconic Symbol of Paris

About the Tower

Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, the Eiffel Tower stands 324 meters tall and was the tallest man-made structure in the world for 41 years. Designed by Gustave Eiffel, this iron lattice tower offers breathtaking views of Paris from its three observation decks.

Inclusions

Experience Highlights

  • Panoramic views from three levels (57m, 115m, 276m)
  • Glass floor on the first level
  • Champagne bar at the top
  • Evening light show every hour after dusk
  • Historical exhibits about the tower's construction

Know before you go

  • Open daily from 9:00 AM to 12:45 AM
  • Last entry at 11:45 PM (midnight in summer)
  • Security checks required - no large bags
  • Best time to visit: Early morning or late evening
  • Stairs available to first and second levels

Not included

  • Guided tours (available for additional fee)
  • Food and drinks at tower restaurants
  • Souvenirs from gift shops
  • Transportation to the tower

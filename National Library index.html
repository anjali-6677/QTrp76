National Library of Kolkata

Explore And Enrich Your Knowledge

About the Experience

The National Library of Kolkata is India's largest library by volume and a repository of knowledge for scholars and book lovers. Spread over a vast expanse, it offers an extensive collection of books, manuscripts, and rare documents. Visitors can immerse themselves in a quiet reading experience or explore historical archives that narrate India's rich literary heritage.

Inclusions

  • Access to the reading rooms
  • Extensive collection of books, manuscripts, and periodicals
  • Reference assistance
  • Free parking for visitors

Know before you go

  • Membership or temporary visitor pass may be required for access
  • Silence must be maintained inside the library premises
  • Photography and videography are restricted in certain areas
  • Children must be accompanied by parents or guardians at all times
  • Valid photo ID is mandatory for entry

Not included

  • Personal study materials
  • Guided tours
  • Printing and photocopy services

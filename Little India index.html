Little India, Singapore

Vibrant Indian Cultural Enclave

About the Attraction

Little India is one of Singapore's most colorful and vibrant ethnic neighborhoods, where the sights, sounds and aromas of India come alive. This bustling district features authentic Indian restaurants, spice shops, flower garland vendors, goldsmiths, and beautifully decorated Hindu temples.

Key Features

  • Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple (ornate Hindu temple)
  • Mustafa Centre (24-hour shopping mall)
  • Tekka Centre (hawker center and wet market)
  • Serangoon Road (main thoroughfare)
  • House of Tan Teng Niah (colorful landmark)

Visitor Information

  • Best time to visit: Evenings and weekends
  • Market hours: Most shops open 10am-10pm
  • Temple visiting hours: Typically 6am-noon & 5-9pm
  • Try local specialties: roti prata, biryani
  • Deepavali festival period is especially vibrant

Getting There

  • MRT: Little India Station (NE7/DT12)
  • Bus services: 23, 64, 65, 66, 67, 131, 139
  • 15-minute walk from Bugis area

Marine Drive Experience

Mumbai's Iconic Queen's Necklace

About Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a 3.6-kilometer-long boulevard in South Mumbai that forms a graceful C-shape along the Arabian Sea coast. Known as the 'Queen's Necklace' because of its string of street lights that resemble a necklace when viewed at night, this iconic promenade is a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike. The art deco buildings lining the drive, the sound of waves crashing against the tetrapods, and the cool sea breeze make Marine Drive the perfect place to experience Mumbai's charm.

Experience Highlights

  • Stunning views of the Arabian Sea and Mumbai skyline
  • Beautiful sunset and nighttime illumination views
  • Art Deco architecture along the boulevard
  • Popular jogging and walking track for fitness enthusiasts
  • Nearby attractions include Chowpatty Beach and Nariman Point

Know before you go

  • Best time to visit: Early morning or evening
  • Very crowded on weekends and holidays
  • Limited seating available along the promenade
  • Several food options available nearby
  • Parking can be challenging during peak hours

Not recommended

  • Swimming in the sea (dangerous currents)
  • Visiting during monsoon high tides
  • Leaving valuables unattended
  • Littering - use designated trash bins

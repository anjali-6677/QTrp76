Dubai Mall Experience

World's Largest Shopping Destination

About the Mall

Dubai Mall is the world's largest shopping mall with over 1,200 retail stores, an Olympic-sized ice rink, the massive Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, and direct access to the Burj Khalifa. This entertainment and shopping complex attracts over 80 million visitors annually with its mix of luxury brands, entertainment options, and dining experiences.

Attractions Included

  • Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo
  • VR Park and KidZania
  • Fashion Avenue luxury shopping
  • Dubai Ice Rink
  • Access to Burj Khalifa and Dubai Fountain

Know before you go

  • Open daily from 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM
  • Friday hours: 10:00 AM to 1:00 AM
  • Free entry to mall, attractions priced separately
  • Valet parking available
  • Tax-free shopping for tourists

Not included

  • Individual attraction tickets
  • Food and beverages
  • Personal shopping expenses

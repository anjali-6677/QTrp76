Langkawi Islands

Tropical Paradise with Duty-Free Shopping

About the Islands

Langkawi is an archipelago of 99 islands known for beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and duty-free shopping. This UNESCO Geopark offers stunning landscapes from the SkyCab cable car to mangrove river tours, making it ideal for both relaxation and adventure.

Key Attractions

  • Langkawi Sky Bridge (iconic curved bridge)
  • Cenang Beach (main tourist area)
  • Kilim Geoforest Park (mangrove tours)
  • Dataran Lang (eagle square)
  • Underwater World Langkawi

Travel Tips

  • Best time: November-March (dry season)
  • Rent a car/scooter to explore the island
  • Duty-free alcohol and chocolates available
  • Bargain at local markets
  • Sun protection is essential

Getting There

  • Langkawi International Airport
  • Ferries from Penang/Kuala Perlis
  • Domestic flights from KL/Penang

Howrah Bridge

An Iconic Landmark of Kolkata

About the Experience

Howrah Bridge is a historic cantilever bridge that spans the Hooghly River, connecting the cities of Howrah and Kolkata. Built in 1943, it is one of the busiest bridges in the world, carrying thousands of vehicles and pedestrians daily. The bridge is an architectural marvel and a symbol of Kolkataâ€™s rich heritage, offering stunning views of the river and the city skyline.

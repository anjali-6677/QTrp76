Victoria Memorial

Explore And Experience History

About the Experience

The Victoria Memorial, an iconic symbol of Kolkata, stands as a grand monument built in memory of Queen Victoria. This stunning white marble structure, surrounded by lush gardens, houses a museum showcasing British-era artifacts, paintings, and historical manuscripts. Take a leisurely stroll through the vast grounds, admire the intricate architecture, or explore the extensive galleries filled with historical treasures.

Inclusions

  • Entry to Victoria Memorial Museum
  • Access to lush gardens and surrounding pathways
  • Exhibitions featuring historical paintings, manuscripts, and artifacts
  • Guided tours (subject to availability)

Know before you go

  • Photography inside the museum is restricted in certain areas
  • Visitors are required to maintain decorum and follow museum guidelines
  • Entry fees apply for both Indian and foreign visitors
  • Children must be accompanied by parents or guardians at all times
  • A valid photo ID is mandatory for ticket purchases

Not included

  • Private guided tours
  • Pickup and drop
  • Food and beverages

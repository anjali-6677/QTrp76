Sentosa Island, Singapore

Singapore's Premier Island Resort Getaway

About the Attraction

Sentosa Island is a 500-hectare resort island offering beaches, theme parks, luxury hotels, golf courses, and entertainment options. Originally a military fortress, it's now Singapore's playground featuring Universal Studios, Adventure Cove Waterpark, and S.E.A. Aquarium.

Key Features

  • Universal Studios Singapore
  • S.E.A. Aquarium (one of world's largest)
  • Adventure Cove Waterpark
  • Palawan Beach with suspension bridge
  • Sentosa Merlion (iconic statue)

Visitor Information

  • Opening hours: Varies by attraction (typically 10am-7pm)
  • Island admission: SGD 4 (some attractions require separate tickets)
  • Best time to visit: Weekdays to avoid crowds
  • Free beach shuttle available
  • Wear swimwear under clothes for water activities

Getting There

  • Sentosa Express monorail from VivoCity
  • Cable car from Mount Faber
  • Walk via Sentosa Boardwalk
  • Bus services: RWS8, 123, 188R

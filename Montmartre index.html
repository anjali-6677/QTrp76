Montmartre

Paris's Historic Artistic Village

About Montmartre

This hilltop neighborhood was once home to Picasso, Van Gogh, and other famous artists. Today, it retains its village charm with cobblestone streets, sidewalk cafes, and the white-domed Sacré-Cœur Basilica offering panoramic views of Paris.

Experience Highlights

  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica and its panoramic views
  • Place du Tertre artist square
  • Moulin Rouge cabaret
  • Vineyard of Montmartre (only vineyard in Paris)
  • Dali Paris museum

Know before you go

  • Best explored on foot (wear comfortable shoes)
  • Funicular railway available to reach Sacré-Cœur
  • Artists may charge for portraits/sketches
  • Evening visits offer beautiful sunset views
  • Be cautious of street scams near tourist areas

Not included

  • Museum entrance fees
  • Artist commissions
  • Meals at restaurants
  • Moulin Rouge show tickets

White Water Rafting in Goa

Thrilling Adventure on the Mhadei River

About the Experience

Experience the thrill of white water rafting in Goa's Mhadei River, surrounded by the lush Western Ghats. This exciting adventure takes you through grade II and III rapids, offering a perfect mix of adrenaline and scenic beauty. Suitable for beginners and experienced rafters alike, with professional guides ensuring safety throughout the journey.

Experience Highlights

  • 10km rafting route with exciting rapids
  • Professional guides and safety equipment provided
  • Beautiful views of the Western Ghats
  • Swimming opportunities in calm sections
  • Photography stops at scenic locations

Know before you go

  • Best season: June to September (monsoon)
  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Wear comfortable clothes that can get wet
  • Bring change of clothes and towel
  • Not recommended for pregnant women or those with back problems

Not included

  • Transportation to the starting point
  • Meals (unless specified in package)
  • Personal expenses
  • Professional photos (available for purchase)

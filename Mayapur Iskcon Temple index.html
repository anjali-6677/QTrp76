Mayapur ISKCON Temple

Experience Divine Peace and Spiritual Bliss

About the Experience

Mayapur ISKCON Temple, a spiritual haven, is nestled along the serene banks of the Ganges. This sacred site offers a unique blend of devotion, culture, and tranquility. Spread across a vast area, the temple features stunning architecture, serene gardens, and devotional activities. Engage in soul-stirring kirtans, witness mesmerizing deity darshans, and immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance. For those seeking deeper knowledge, the temple also hosts enlightening discourses and meditation sessions.

Inclusions

  • Temple darshan and spiritual discourses
  • Participation in kirtans and bhajans
  • Access to gardens and meditation spaces
  • Entry to the Vedic Planetarium and museum
  • Free prasadam (sanctified food) during lunch hours

Know before you go

  • Modest and traditional attire is required inside the temple premises
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas
  • Silence and discipline must be maintained during prayers and discourses
  • Footwear is not allowed inside the temple; designated shoe racks are available
  • A valid photo ID may be required for special entry or accommodations

Not included

  • Private guided tours
  • Transport services (Pickup and drop-off)
  • Accommodation and personal expenses

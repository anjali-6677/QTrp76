Dakshineswar Temple

Seek Blessings and Find Peace

About the Experience

Dakshineswar Kali Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, situated on the eastern bank of the Hooghly River. Built in the 19th century by Rani Rashmoni, the temple is known for its spiritual significance and historical architecture. Pilgrims can meditate in the serene ambiance, witness the sacred rituals, and explore the temple complex, which includes the chambers where Sri Ramakrishna attained spiritual enlightenment.

Inclusions

  • Entry to the temple premises
  • Darshan and Aarti experience
  • Access to the temple gardens and ghats
  • Guided spiritual discourse on request

Know before you go

  • Photography inside the temple sanctum is not allowed
  • Visitors must maintain silence and decorum within the premises
  • Appropriate traditional attire is required for entry
  • Temple doors close during afternoon hours; plan your visit accordingly
  • Footwear must be removed before entering the temple premises

Not included

  • Prasad and religious offerings
  • Personal puja arrangements
  • Transport to and from the temple

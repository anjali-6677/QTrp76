Heritage Monument Tour

Explore And Witness History

About the Experience

Step back in time with our guided tour of this magnificent historical monument, a UNESCO World Heritage Site dating back to the 12th century. Our expert guides will take you through the architectural marvels, hidden passages, and fascinating stories behind this iconic structure. Learn about the ancient craftsmanship, historical events, and cultural significance of this awe-inspiring monument that has stood the test of time.

Inclusions

  • Expert historian guide (multiple language options)
  • Full access to all public areas of the monument
  • Audio guide with detailed commentary
  • Interactive map of the monument complex
  • Complimentary bottled water

Know before you go

  • Tour duration: Approximately 2.5 hours
  • Moderate walking required (comfortable shoes recommended)
  • No flash photography allowed in interior spaces
  • Security check mandatory for all visitors
  • Monument closes at sunset

Not included

  • Transportation to/from the monument
  • Meals and additional refreshments
  • Special access to restricted areas
  • Souvenirs and personal purchases

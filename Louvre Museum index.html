Louvre Museum

World's Largest Art Museum

About the Museum

Housed in the Louvre Palace, this iconic museum spans over 72,735 square meters and contains approximately 38,000 objects from prehistory to the 21st century. The glass pyramid entrance, designed by I.M. Pei, has become nearly as famous as the artworks inside.

Collection Highlights

  • Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci
  • Venus de Milo (ancient Greek statue)
  • Winged Victory of Samothrace
  • Code of Hammurabi
  • Coronation of Napoleon painting

Know before you go

  • Open Wednesday to Monday (closed Tuesdays)
  • Hours: 9AM-6PM (until 9:45PM on Fridays)
  • Free admission first Saturday evening each month
  • Skip-the-line tickets highly recommended
  • Plan at least 3-4 hours for main highlights

Burj Khalifa Experience

World's Tallest Building

About the Tower

The Burj Khalifa is a stunning architectural marvel standing at 828 meters (2,717 feet), making it the tallest building in the world. Opened in 2010, this iconic skyscraper features the highest outdoor observation deck (At the Top - 148th floor), luxurious residences, corporate suites, and the Armani Hotel. The tower's design was inspired by Islamic architecture and the desert flower Hymenocallis, with its Y-shaped floor plan providing optimal residential and hotel space.

Experience Highlights

  • At the Top observation decks on 124th, 125th and 148th floors
  • 360-degree views of Dubai and beyond
  • Multimedia presentations about Dubai's development
  • Sunset and night-time viewing experiences
  • Proximity to Dubai Mall and Dubai Fountain

Know before you go

  • Opening hours: 8:30 AM to 11:00 PM daily
  • Best time to visit: Sunset for day and night views
  • Security checks similar to airport screening
  • Advance booking recommended (especially for sunset slots)
  • Dress code: Smart casual (no beachwear)

Not included

  • Transportation to Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall
  • Food and beverages at Atmosphere restaurant
  • Professional photographs (available for purchase)
  • Access to private residences or hotel areas

About the Experience

Eden Gardens, one of the most iconic cricket stadiums in the world, offers an unforgettable experience for sports lovers. Located in the heart of Kolkata, this historic venue has hosted numerous legendary matches. Explore the grandeur of the stadium, witness the breathtaking view from the stands, and feel the electrifying atmosphere that defines cricket in India.

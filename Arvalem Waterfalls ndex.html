Arvalem Waterfalls

The Hidden Gem of Goa

About the Waterfalls

Arvalem Waterfalls, also known as Harvalem Waterfalls, is a picturesque cascade located in North Goa, near the town of Bicholim. The waterfall drops from a height of about 50 meters into a natural pool, surrounded by lush greenery. Nearby attractions include the Arvalem Caves and Rudreshwar Temple, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Experience Highlights

  • Beautiful waterfall with natural swimming pool
  • Ancient Arvalem Caves (Pandava Caves) nearby
  • Rudreshwar Temple with Shiva linga
  • Peaceful surroundings ideal for picnics
  • Photography opportunities with the falls and caves

Know before you go

  • Best time to visit: Post-monsoon (October to December)
  • Water flow reduces significantly in summer
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking
  • Carry drinking water and snacks
  • Changing rooms available near the falls

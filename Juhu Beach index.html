Juhu Beach Experience

Mumbai's Most Iconic Beachfront

About Juhu Beach

Juhu Beach is one of Mumbai's most popular beaches, known for its vibrant atmosphere, delicious street food, and beautiful sunsets. Stretching about 6 km along the Arabian Sea, this beach is a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike. Enjoy horse rides along the shore, try famous Mumbai street foods like bhelpuri and pav bhaji, or simply relax and watch the sunset over the Arabian Sea. The beach comes alive in the evenings with performers, food stalls, and families enjoying the sea breeze.

Beach Highlights

  • Evening street food stalls serving Mumbai specialties
  • Horse and camel rides along the shore
  • Beautiful sunset views over the Arabian Sea
  • Weekend cultural performances and activities
  • Proximity to celebrity homes and film studios

Know before you go

  • Best time to visit: Early morning or evening (avoid noon heat)
  • Beach is crowded on weekends and holidays
  • Swimming not recommended due to strong currents
  • Bargain for prices at food stalls and activities
  • Keep valuables secure due to pickpockets

Not recommended

  • Swimming in the sea (dangerous currents)
  • Visiting during high tide
  • Carrying expensive jewelry or large amounts of cash
  • Leaving belongings unattended

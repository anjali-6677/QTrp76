Shri Bhagvati Temple

Ancient Temple Dedicated to Goddess Bhagvati

About the Temple

Shri Bhagvati Temple in Pernem, North Goa, is one of the most revered temples in the state, dedicated to Goddess Bhagvati, an incarnation of Goddess Parvati. The temple showcases beautiful Goan temple architecture with its dome-shaped structure, deepstambh (lamp tower), and intricate wood carvings. The annual Jatra (festival) attracts thousands of devotees.

Temple Highlights

  • Beautiful Goan-style temple architecture
  • Sacred pond within the temple complex
  • Annual Jatra festival in January/February
  • Peaceful atmosphere for meditation
  • Traditional Goan Hindu rituals

Know before you go

  • Open daily from 6:00 AM to 8:30 PM
  • Dress modestly (shoulders and knees covered)
  • Remove footwear before entering temple premises
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas
  • Best time to visit: Early morning or evening aarti
